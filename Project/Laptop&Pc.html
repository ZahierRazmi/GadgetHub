Laptop / PC

Manufacturer: Lenovo, HP, Asus, Acer

Lenovo

Lenovo laptops include the business-oriented ThinkPad series, versatile Yoga series with 2-in-1 designs, consumer-friendly IdeaPad series, and gaming-centric Legion series. They are known for quality, innovation, and diverse options.

Laptop Line-Up

Lenovo desktop PCs encompass business-focused ThinkCentre series, consumer-oriented IdeaCentre series, gaming-centric Legion series, and compact Tiny-in-One series. They offer a range of options from traditional towers to all-in-one solutions, addressing various user needs.

PC Line-Up

HP

HP laptops are versatile and cover a broad range of user needs. The HP Spectre series emphasizes premium design and performance, the HP Envy series combines style with high-end features, and the HP Pavilion series offers reliable performance for everyday use. The Omen series caters to gaming enthusiasts with powerful configurations. HP laptops are known for their quality build and innovative features.

Laptop Line-Up

HP desktop PCs include the HP Pavilion series, offering a balance between performance and affordability for general users. The HP Envy series combines style and functionality in a desktop form factor, while the HP Omen series targets gamers with high-performance hardware. HP desktops provide options for various use cases, from everyday computing to gaming and content creation.

PC Line-Up

Asus

ASUS laptops are known for their innovation and performance. The ASUS ZenBook series emphasizes sleek designs and high-end features, ideal for professionals. The ASUS VivoBook series offers a balance of performance and affordability for everyday users, and the ASUS ROG (Republic of Gamers) series is dedicated to gaming, featuring powerful configurations and gaming-centric designs. ASUS laptops are recognized for their build quality and diverse options.

Laptop Line-Up

ASUS desktop PCs include various series to meet different user requirements. The ASUS Vivo series caters to general users, providing a combination of performance and value. The ASUS ROG (Republic of Gamers) series focuses on gaming desktops, offering powerful hardware configurations for an immersive gaming experience. ASUS desktops often feature stylish designs and customizable options to appeal to a broad range of users.

PC Line-up

Acer

Acer laptops provide a diverse range catering to different user needs. The Aspire series is designed for everyday use, the Predator series is tailored for gaming enthusiasts with high-performance configurations, and the Swift series focuses on sleek and portable designs, often favored by professionals. Acer laptops are known for offering a balance between affordability and performance.

Laptop Line-Up

Acer desktop PCs cover a spectrum of requirements. The Aspire series includes budget-friendly options for general use, while the Predator series is geared towards gamers, featuring powerful hardware configurations. Acer's desktops provide options for various user preferences, from compact form factors to traditional towers.
