Apple.Inc

The iPhone is a line of smartphones produced by Apple Inc. that use Apple's own iOS mobile operating system. The first-generation iPhone was announced by then–Apple CEO Steve Jobs on January 9, 2007. Since then, Apple has annually released new iPhone models and iOS updates. As of November 1, 2018, more than 2.2 billion iPhones had been sold. As of 2022, the iPhone accounts for 15.6% of global smartphone market share.[3] The iPhone was the first mobile phone to use multi-touch technology.[4] Since the iPhone's launch, it has gained larger screen sizes, video-recording, waterproofing, and many accessibility features. Up to the iPhone 8 and 8 Plus, iPhones had a single button on the front panel, with the iPhone 5s and later integrating a Touch ID fingerprint sensor.[5] Since the iPhone X, iPhone models have switched to a nearly bezel-less front screen design with Face ID facial recognition, and app switching activated by gestures. Touch ID is still used for the budget iPhone SE series.

Iphone line-up 2024

The iPad, created by Apple Inc., stands as a pinnacle of tablet innovation, seamlessly blending form and function. Renowned for its sleek design and Retina display technology, the iPad offers users a visually immersive experience with vibrant colors and crisp graphics. Powered by Apple's custom-designed processors, iPads deliver robust performance for a wide array of applications, from productivity tools to creative pursuits. The versatility of the iPad is further enriched by accessories like the Apple Pencil and Smart Keyboard, expanding its utility for tasks like drawing and typing. With a rich app ecosystem, long battery life, and a focus on security features, the iPad has become a symbol of portable computing excellence, finding its place in education, business, and entertainment realms. As a testament to its enduring popularity, the iPad continues to redefine the landscape of tablet technology.

Samsung.Inc

Samsung is a major player in the smartphone industry, offering a wide range of devices to cater to different needs. The Galaxy S series represents their flagship line, known for cutting-edge technology and premium features. The Galaxy Note series combines a large display with the S Pen stylus, targeting users who seek productivity. Additionally, the Galaxy A and Galaxy M series provide options across various price points, ensuring a diverse market presence.

Smartphone line-up 2024

Samsung's tablet lineup includes the Galaxy Tab series, offering devices suitable for both productivity and entertainment. The Galaxy Tab S series is known for its high-end specifications and AMOLED displays, making them ideal for multimedia consumption. The Galaxy Tab A series provides more budget-friendly options, balancing performance and affordability. Overall, Samsung tablets cater to a wide audience with varying preferences.

Huawei

Huawei is recognized for its innovative smartphone lineup, particularly the Huawei P and Mate series. The P series emphasizes photography capabilities, often featuring advanced camera systems and sleek designs. The Mate series focuses on high-performance devices with large displays, catering to users who prioritize productivity. Huawei has also introduced the Honor series, which offers competitive features at more affordable prices. Notably, Huawei smartphones often feature their own Kirin processors and advanced camera technologies.

Smartphone line-up 2024

Huawei's tablet lineup includes the MediaPad series, which combines sleek design with powerful performance. The MediaPad M and T series cater to different needs, with the M series focusing on high-end features, and the T series providing more budget-friendly options. Huawei tablets often feature high-resolution displays, long-lasting batteries, and support for stylus input. The company aims to provide a diverse range of tablets to accommodate various user preferences.
